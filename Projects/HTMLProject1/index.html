Website Critiques

Utah National Parks

Question #1:

What was your first impression of this site?

Answer:

My first impression is that it's very well put together and easy to look at, it's not hard on the eyes.

Question #2:

How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)

Answer:

Yes they have the links to the different national parks and trip planning on the left and then they have the rest of the information on the right side.

Question #3:

Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?

Answer:

The sites structure makes sense to me because it's not cluttered andd it's all in a good layout.

Question #4:

Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?

Answer:

No I don't think it does, it's not in a color that doesn't pop out or blend in too much and I feel like it enhances it by choosing complementary colors.

Question #5:

How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?

Answer:

The load time was fine for me it was nearly instant.

Question #6:

Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?

Answer:

Yeah the text color didn't blend into the background nor was it a hard color to look at.

Question #7:

Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?

Answer:

Yeah the site had other tabs at the top and a search bar that you can use to navigate to look at other things.

Question #8:

Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?

Answer:

Yeah I feel like the layouts and color schemes were mainly the exact same with a few differences an exceptions.

Question #9:

How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?

Answer:

The site responds really well to changes in the window sizes

Question #10:

What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Answer:

One thing that I found interesting is that it has an events tab that when you click it, it redirects you to another website made by a different company.
